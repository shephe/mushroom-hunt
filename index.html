Mushroom Hunt

Welcome to Mushroom Hunt! The object of the game is to find 10 mushrooms by clicking on the squares in the forest. Players alternate turns, and a round is over when all the mushrooms on the grid have been found. Beware of the death cap - if you find it, your basket will be emptied of all the mushrooms you've found so far!
